Introducing Color Catcher — Our First Game

We're thrilled to announce the launch of Color Catcher, a fun catching game for kids ages 4 and up. Guide your basket to catch falling stars, gems, treats, and surprises while dodging obstacles.

Color Catcher was built with one goal: let kids play without parents worrying. That means zero ads, zero data collection, no in-app purchases, and full offline support. One price — $0.99 — and you're done.

Three difficulty levels grow with your child, from Easy (simple catching, slow pace) to Hard (faster drops, more obstacles, and power-ups to master). A local leaderboard lets siblings and friends compete, and player profiles mean everyone gets their own space.

Color Catcher coming soon on Android and iOS. Learn more on the Color Catcher page.
